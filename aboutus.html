About Us

From Antipolo, For Every Rider

AlecShiro Bikeshop was established in 2021 and is located at Brgy. Inarawan, Padilla, Antipolo City near the Inarawan Church. The shop is registered under Harold Miranda while the business operations are currently managed by his daughter.

The bikeshop serves bike enthusiasts, owners, and customers looking for quality bike parts, accessories, and repair services. AlecShiro Bikeshop is also active online through Facebook and TikTok for easier communication with customers.

Mission

Our mission is to provide affordable and quality biking products and services while supporting the growing cycling community. We aim to deliver excellent customer service both in-store and online.

Vision

AlecShiro Bikeshop aims to expand its business by opening branches in Paenaan and Masinag while creating a strong online presence through a modern and user-friendly website for faster and easier transactions.
